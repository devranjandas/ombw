The heart of the workshop

At its core: Sudarshan Kriya

Everything in these four mornings builds toward one powerful breathing technique — Sudarshan Kriya. Using specific natural rhythms of the breath, it releases deep-seated stress and tension, and leaves you feeling calm, clear, and deeply energised.

  • A science-backed practice that heals from within
  • Calms the mind and lifts your mood, naturally
  • Boosts immunity and resilience against stress

Once you learn it, it's yours for life — a few minutes a day to reset and recharge.
